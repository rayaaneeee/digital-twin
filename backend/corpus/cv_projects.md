## AI & Data Science Projects — مشاريع الذكاء الاصطناعي وعلوم البيانات

### Threat Intelligence RAG Copilot — استخبارات التهديدات بتقنية RAG
Real-time AI threat intelligence assistant ingesting 4 live security databases.
- GitHub: https://github.com/rayaaneeee/threat-intelligence-copilot
- Live: https://rytaaaaaaa-threat-intelligence-copilot.hf.space
- Data sources: NVD (7,700+ CVEs), MITRE ATT&CK (697 techniques), CISA KEV (1,627 exploited), EPSS scores
- Features: Analyst Chat (RAG + Groq streaming), Stack Analyzer (agentic tool-use loop), Live Dashboard (ApexCharts)
- Tech: Vue 3, FastAPI, ChromaDB, SQLite knowledge graph, Groq llama-3.3-70b, Docker

### DermAI — Medical Imaging with Explainability
Production-quality skin lesion classification with Grad-CAM heatmaps, confidence calibration, and uncertainty quantification.
- EfficientNet-B0 fine-tuned on ISIC 2019 dataset, achieving AUC-ROC 0.9630 across 7 classes.
- Deployed on HuggingFace Spaces.
- Tech: PyTorch, EfficientNet-B0, Grad-CAM/Grad-CAM++/EigenCAM, Temperature Scaling, MC Dropout, FastAPI, Docker

### Intelligent OCR & NLP Document Extraction Pipeline — Temacina
Built an OCR and NLP pipeline to extract and structure unstructured B2B exhibitor data at scale (12,000+ records).
- Tech: Google Cloud Vision API, NLP, Hugging Face Transformers, Python, Django, Vue.js

### Pain Location & Severity Prediction — Behavioral Data Study
Collected a dataset of student behavioral indicators and trained multiple ML models to predict pain location and level.
- Tech: Python, Scikit-learn, Classification & Regression Modeling

### Multivariate Time Series Forecasting — Kaggle Challenge
Built and tuned forecasting and classification models for a competitive Kaggle time series challenge.
- Tech: Python, Time Series Analysis

### Financial Price Crisis Prediction
Developed a time series model to anticipate financial price crises from historical market data.
- Tech: Python, Time Series Analysis

### Multi-Drone Delivery Route Optimization
Designed an end-to-end multi-drone delivery system optimizing routes, speeds, and altitudes.
- Compared MILP against Ant Colony Optimization, Genetic Algorithm, and Simulated Annealing — SA achieved the best results.
- Tech: Python, MILP, Ant Colony Optimization, Genetic Algorithms, Simulated Annealing
